Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

VCG Recordz ("we", "us") is an independent music label based in Valleiry. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us through this website. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the French Data Protection Act.

2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal information is VCG Recordz, headquartered in Valleiry, Haute-Savoie. For any question regarding the processing of your data, please contact us at team@vcgrecordz.eu.

3. Data We Collect

We only collect data that you voluntarily provide through the contact form on the "Get in touch" page. This includes your first name, last name, email address, phone number (optional) and the content of your message. We do not collect any sensitive personal data. We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics services.

4. Purpose & Legal Basis

Your data is processed solely to respond to your requests · collaborations, distribution, booking, licensing or general inquiries. The legal basis for this processing is your explicit consent (Article 6.1.a GDPR), given when you submit the contact form and accept this Privacy Policy.

5. Retention Period

Contact form data is kept for a maximum of three (3) years from the last interaction with you. After this period, your data is either deleted or anonymised. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

6. Third Parties & Subprocessors

Contact form submissions are processed through Formspree (Formspree Inc., USA) which acts as our subprocessor. Formspree is GDPR-compliant and uses Standard Contractual Clauses for international data transfers. We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with any other third party for marketing purposes.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data, as well as the right to data portability and the right to withdraw your consent at any time. To exercise these rights, please email us at team@vcgrecordz.eu. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the French data protection authority (CNIL, www.cnil.fr).

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration or destruction. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the date of the latest version. Material changes will be communicated prominently on the site.

10. Contact

For any question or request related to this Privacy Policy, please contact us at team@vcgrecordz.eu.

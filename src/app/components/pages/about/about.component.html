About Us

Founded in the vibrant currents of music and entrepreneurial spirit, VCG Recordz's journey began in 2013 when a young Kitsui, at the tender age of 13, started dabbling in music production. His passion quickly escalated as he explored various recording and music composition software, including Cubase, Logic Pro X, FL Studio, and Reaper.

The year 2018 marked a significant turning point when Kitsui met Køni and shortly thereafter, Biggy. By 2019, Kitsui and Køni were not just acquaintances but collaborators, bonding over their shared love for music, rapping, and singing together. Kitsui, who had honed his skills in voice mixing, took under his wing the task of mixing both his and Køni’s vocals. This dynamic duo spent countless hours—days, weeks, and months—creating music, especially during the 2020 Covid-19 lockdowns, always with Biggy by their side.

As their skills sharpened, Kitsui passed on his knowledge of mixing to Køni, empowering him to record autonomously and master his vocal finesse using professional plugins and custom presets. This led to an influx of requests from other artists seeking their mixing magic, but without a formal structure, they were initially unable to take on external projects.

Realizing the potential, and driven by a staunch determination to remain independent, the idea of forming their own label took root. In 2021, conversations began to shape this vision, culminating in the official launch of VCG Recordz on January 16, 2024.

At the helm of the company stood Kitsui, Køni, and Biggy, with Biggy honored as the president for his unwavering support and solidarity from the very start—not just in music but in life and business endeavors. Despite Biggy’s title, Kitsui managed the day-to-day operations, including finances, accounting, web development, and client management, while Køni and he jointly steered the business decisions.

Biggy also ventured into videography, having filmed all their amateur music videos since 2019 and starring in their first official music video in 2022. The label grew to include beatmakers like OGS and Shidoz, expanding their reach and influence in the music industry. VCG Recordz also launched VCG Production, a platform on YouTube and Beatstar showcasing and selling beats crafted by their talented team.

Today, VCG Recordz stands as a hub for artists seeking to collaborate and innovate within the music industry. Operating from two home studios, the label not only produces and mixes their own material but also extends these services to a wide array of local and national artists, steadfastly supporting a vision of creativity, independence, and collective artistic effort.
